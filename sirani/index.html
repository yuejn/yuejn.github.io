Elisabetta Sirani (1638-1665)

Elisabetta Sirani (1638-1665) was a prolific Baroque painter and printmaker from seventeenth century Bologna, Italy.

Works

Sirani produced more than 200 paintings in her short lifetime. I have tried to source her works that are not in private collections.
