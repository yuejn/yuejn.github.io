Sauce for Chinese broccoli

Published: Jan 2020 ⋄ 1 min read
food

This is a great sauce we go back to! With thanks to this recipe for crispy tofu and broccoli stir fry.

  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 60 ml of water
  • 60 ml light soy sauce
