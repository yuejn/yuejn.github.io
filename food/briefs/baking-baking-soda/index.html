Baking baking soda

Published: Feb 2021 ⋄ 1 min read
food

Creating an alkaline agent at home: spread 100 grams of baking soda on parchment paper. Place in oven at 120 degrees Celsius for an hour. The baking soda should reduce by 2/3rds of its weight (so, at 100 grams, should become 66 grams). This is caustic so pls be careful and don’t touch it with your bare hands.
