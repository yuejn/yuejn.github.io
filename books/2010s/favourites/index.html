Favourite books from the past decade (2010s)

Published: Nov 2019 ⋄ 1 min read
books

A small list of moving, life-changing, and/or enjoyable books I’ve read this past decade:

  • Know My Name (2019) by Chanel Miller

  • Why I’m No Longer Talking To White People About Race (2017) by Reni Eddo-Lodge

  • When Breath Comes Air (2016) by Paul Kalanithi

  • On Living (2016) by Kerry Egan

  • Wave (2013) by Sonali Deraniyagala

  • Chasing the Flame: One Man’s Fight to Save the World (2008) by Samantha Power

  • The Housekeeper and the Professor (2003) by Yōko Ogawa

  • Purple Hibiscus (2003) by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

  • The Magus (1965) by John Fowles
