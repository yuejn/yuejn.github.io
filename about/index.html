About

Last updated: 2019

Hello there!

Welcome to this intentionally unstructured and disorganised personal website. Instead of keeping a private journal, I thought it would be interesting — a little throwback, if you will — to publish random thoughts and such online for friends and, following the nature of the internet, strangers. It is a digital garden; of what Christopher Biscardi calls “lowercase b blogging…It’s writing in small, unfinished pieces…”. (I also really like Joel Hooks' more longer explanation.)

I read History with a focus on humanitarianism and have been working in the Netherlands since 2015 in the spaces of community, sustainability, and sports. At present, I am a software engineer in Amsterdam. It was an unplanned journey:

I am particuarly good at impersonating a Milanese accent. I am comfortably okay on the bicycle. I excel at English-language pub quizzes. I am on occasional, but rather terribly awkward, podcast guest. I like pastel de queijo, taking photographs, and bad movies — sometimes all at once.
