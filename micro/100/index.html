Helplessness

Last updated: 2020

I think one of this age’s strangest things is witnessing the world around us implode – in real-time, on fire, with ego. And then we wake up in the morning, eat breakfast, and go to work. It is surreal.

What do we do? What can we do? How do we do it? How can we better help the most vulnerable, the most marginalised? History has told us they will suffer first and the most. How can we lobby those at the top to effect change?
