A month-by-month list of events that usually happen in Amsterdam and surrounding areas. Check the event’s website for more up-to-date information! And this is totally not a comprehensive list – a mix of theatre, art, film, liquor, food, and music.

January

  • First Art Fair
  • L & B Whiskey Weekend
  • National Tulip Day
  • Hotel Nacht, an evening where a number of hotels offer reduced rates to Amsterdammers to spend the night
  • Vondelparkloop, a race around Vondelpark
  • Ice * Amsterdam, an outdoor ice-rink on Museumplein
  • every third Sunday of the month, begins Museum Markt

February

  • Impro Amsterdam, an international improv festival
  • Little Italy highlights Italian food, drink, and music
  • Mono Japan celebrates contemporary Japanese craft and design
  • Human Rights Weekend, participate in a weekend of films, discussions, and photography (English)
  • Huishoudbeurs, a homemakers’ fair
  • Chocoa Festival brings together the whole chocolate supply chain from cocoa farmers to cocoa consumers
  • Opera Forward Festival is two weeks of new works, original initiatives, and young talents of Dutch National Opera

March

  • Jazzfest, for Amsterdam jazz musicians from different schools under one roof
  • The Whiskey & Spirits Festival
  • The Amsterdam Coffee Festival
  • CinemAsia Film Festival, enhancing Asian visibility in culture and media
  • Cinedans - Dance on Screen Festival
  • Something Raw, international dance and performance festival
  • Roze Filmdagen, LGBTQ film festival
  • Cross-linx Festival, a travelling indie-classical festival where avant-garde pop and contemporary music meet
  • Amsterdam Wine Festival
  • Open Tower Day where the public is able to visit many of the city’s monumental towers
  • Goois Jazz Festival
  • Pure Markt, where artisan entrepreneurs go to talk and sell good food and drinks with a little arts, crafts, and music thrown in
  • National Restaurant Week is a time when you can enjoy a special 3-course dinner for less than €30,00

April

It’s flower season!

  • Tulip Festival is on for the whole month
  • Minimal Music Festival
  • The Cherry Blossom Festival in Amsterdamse Bos runs for two weeks to mark the coming of spring
  • IAmExpat Fair
  • National Museum Week
  • Sounds of the Underground Festival for obscure, hardcore, and psychedlic music
  • Imagine Film Festival
  • Record Store Day, a global intimate music event
  • World Press Photo Exhibition at De Nieuwe Kerk
  • Kunst RAI Art, an art fair
  • from 21st: the Springsnow Festival celebrating the city’s whirling elm seeds
  • on the 26th is King’s Night and on the 27th is King’s Day

May

  • In May, the Openluchttheatre (open air theatre) starts in Vondelpark through the whole month
  • 4 en 5 mei is the city’s commemoration of World War II
  • International Gin Festival
  • Moving Futures, Dans Festival is a travelling, innovative dance festival
  • Lente Kabinet is a spring music festival
  • Rollende Keukens is a popular food truck festival taking place over a long weekend
  • Taste of Amsterdam, a festival for foodies

June

  • ZOOmeravonden start at Artis Zoo, staying open till sunset
  • Sneakerness a conference for sneakerheads
  • Red Light Jazz, a jazz festival in the Red Light District
  • Open Tuinen Dagen (Open Garden Days) is a weekend where the public can visit the gardens of the stately houses on the canal belt
  • Houtfestival
  • Nomads Festival
  • Holland Festival is a leading international performing arts festival
  • Robeco Summer Nights

July

It is music festival season…

  • Keti Koti Festival celebrates the abolition of slavery in Suriname
  • Kwaku Summer Festival, a multicultural event with performances, dance, music, and football
  • Over het IJ, location theatre and installations
  • Cineblend Festival, documentaries on cultural diversity

August

  • Pride Amsterdam, street parties and world-famous canal parade
  • Grachten Festival, young musicians from around the world perform across the city
  • Haarlem Jazz and More
  • Summer Dance Forever, an inner-city urban dance festival with participants from all around the world
  • Prinsengrachtconcert, the largest classical open-air concert in the Netherlands
  • Latin Village
  • World Cinema Amsterdam Festival
  • Amsterdam Fringe Festival shows theatre and dance of new, unknown, and untamed talent
  • Huiskamer Festival, performances in living rooms
  • Geelvinck Fortepiano Festival, specialised musicians play historical pianos

September

  • Heritage Day
  • Unseen Festival, for the latest ideas, exhibitions, and projects in the world of contemporary photography
  • Amsterdam Fashion Week
  • Amsterdam City Swim
  • Dutch Theatre Festival
  • Dam tot Damloop, a running event from Amsterdam to Zaandam
  • Klassiek Op Het Amstelveld, open air festival in the Amstelveld
  • Arts & Antiques Weekend

October

  • Muziek in het seven bruggen kwartier, small-scale chamber music festivals in beautiful, unusual locations
  • ADE, the Amsterdam Dance Event (a festival and a conference)
  • Camera Japan, for Japanese films and workshops
  • Adnight, an evening where creative agencies across the city open their doors to show off what they do
  • KLIK Amsterdam Animation Festival, family-oriented programming
  • Read My World, for world literature
  • Cello Biennale
  • TCS Amsterdam Marathon

November

  • Bacchus Wijn Festival
  • Museumnacht, an evening where museums are open till late with parties and special tours and activities
  • International Documentary Film Festival
  • Storytelling Festival — national and international storytellers (and listeners)
  • Amsterdam Art Weekend
  • On the 17th, Sinterklaas arrives in Amsterdam

December

  • Beginning of the Amsterdam Light Festival until the end of January where light installations grace the canals
  • IQMF, the queer & migrant film festival
  • On the 5th is Sinterklaas
